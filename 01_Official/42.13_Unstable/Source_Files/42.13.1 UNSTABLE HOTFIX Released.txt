This patch is now available on the Unstable Steam Beta.

Instructions: Right-Click game in Steam library -> Properties -> Click “Betas” tab -> Select “Unstable” from the Beta Participation Dropdown Menu.
 

MP save games from 42.13.0 will not be compatible.
